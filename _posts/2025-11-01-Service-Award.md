I was awarded the Postdoctoral Fellow Excellence in Service Award at UMB. It was a great honor to be recognized by the University for all the work I do with outreach to local students. I love interacting with students of all ages, including young kids at an annual halloween event, middle-school kids with a mix of youthful energy and a growing sense of responsibility, and high-schoolers who can imagine trying to address "wicked" problems in society.
