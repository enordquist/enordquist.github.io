--- title: "Physics-based modeling of DnaK-substrate binding" excerpt: "Molecular chaperones like DnaK help fold misfolded proteins,
but how do they distinguish well-folded, misfolded, and disordered regions?
" collection: portfolio ---

I built a model of substrate affinity by training an MD-derived interactions matrix on low-resolution binding affinity data. The resulting model recovers both the binding affinity trends, but also higher resolution information in crystal structures of bound substrates. Read more (accepted, PLOS Comp Bio), or view the code on Github.
