Visualizing Molecular Dynamics: a very powerful tool, essential for success!
[Download](https://www.ks.uiuc.edu/Development/Download/download.cgi?PackageName=VMD) (if needed)
Check out the [documentation](https://www.ks.uiuc.edu/Research/vmd/current/docs.html)

For quick reference, the two most common ways to load files in vmd are to load many "frames" of single system, possibly with multiple files such as: ```vmd topology.psf trajectory.dcd``` or to load many files as separate molecules such as: ```vmd -m *.pdb```
